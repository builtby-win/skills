# Blog

Manage your draft blog posts - review, expand, edit, or publish them.

## What it does

1. Scans for draft blog posts (posts with `draft: true`)
2. Lists pending drafts with titles and dates
3. Lets you select a draft to work on
4. Provides options to expand, edit, publish, or delete

## When to use

- End of day to review captured notes
- When ready to polish and publish a draft
- To check what drafts are pending
- To flesh out quick notes into full blog posts

## Usage

```bash
/blog
```

The command will:
- Find all posts with `draft: true`
- Display them sorted by date
- Let you select which draft to work on
- Guide you through editing or publishing
